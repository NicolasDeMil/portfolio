NICOLAS DE MIL

Hello everyone and welcome to my portfolio :) I've had somewhat of a peculiar journey: I was born in Paris from a French dad and a Filipino mom. I grew up in Tokyo until I was 16 and then moved to Singapore where I finished my secondary education with honors on the French Baccalaureat. I then went on to Switzerland, where I graduated from an International Hospitality Management degree with Honors at the Ecole Hoteliere de Lausanne. During my time at university, I realized my real passions lied in Technology and Games. I learned Python online and after applying to different schools (and to everyone's surprise, including myself,) I got into the Computer Science program at the National University of Singapore. It was an amazing experience, (but unfortunately once again, didn't feel like it was the right place for me.) So after 2 semesters I decided it was time for me to start my career and get a job! I wanted to improve on my weaknesses of communication and overcome my shyness so I moved to London and worked as a Sales Executive in a SaaS Technology firm for one and a half years. I order to get back to my core passions, I resigned and decided to pursue a career as a self taught developper and took online course between working part time jobs at cafes and restaurants. I then found out about a bootcamp that would help me in jumpstarting my career. I immediately applied and 9-weeks later, I'm more ready than I've ever been to start my career in this industry. Now you're all caught up :)
