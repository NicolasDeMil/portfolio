ABOUT ME

Hello everyone and welcome to my portfolio :)

I was born in Paris from French and Filipino parents. I grew up in Tokyo until I was 16 and then moved to Singapore where I finished my secondary education with honors on the French Baccalaureat.
I then went on to the Ecole Hoteliere de Lausanne in Switzerland, where I graduated from an International Hospitality Management degree with Honors. During this time, I realized my real passion lied in Technology and Games. I learned Python online and got into the Computer Science program at the National University of Singapore.
I learned a lot and after 2 semesters I felt it was not the right environment for me to flourish so I decided to get a job that would help improve my weaknesses in communication and help me overcome my shyness.
I moved to London and found work as a Sales Executive in a SaaS Technology firm. After 18 months, it was time to return to my core passions and continue in my career in Tech. I took online courses between working part-time jobs at cafes and restaurants and I completed a full-stack development bootcamp in December 2019.
I am now very excited to find a job doing what I love.

INTERESTS

From a very young age waking up at 6am to get more play time to today, I have always been a massive video game fan. I love the fantasy and the alternate realities but mostly I love the fact that everyone can use them and that they bring people together.

Design is something I've recently come to appreciate working on different projects for the bootcamp. I really enjoyed working on the front-end aspects and see my designs come to life. I've always been interested in the Arts, and am very creative.

PopCulture, Food & Cinema
