NICOLAS DE MIL

Hello everyone and welcome to my portfolio :) I was born in Paris from a French dad and a Filipino mom. I grew up in Tokyo until I was 16 and then moved to Singapore where I finished my secondary education with honors on the French Baccalaureat. I then went on to the Ecole Hoteliere de Lausanne in Switzerland, where I graduated from an International Hospitality Management degree with Honors. During this time, I realized my real passion lied in Technology and Games. I learned Python online and I managed to get into the Computer Science program at the National University of Singapore. It was a great experience however after 2 semesters I felt it was not the right environment for me to flourish. I decided to get a job that would help improve my weaknesses of communication and help me overcome my shyness. I moved to London and found work as a Sales Executive in a SaaS Technology firm. I stayed there for one and a half years however in order to get back to my core passions, I decided to pursue a career as a self taught developper and took online courses between working part-time jobs at cafes and restaurants. I then found out about a bootcamp that would help me in jumpstarting my career as a dev. I immediately applied and 9-weeks later, I'm more ready than I've ever been to start my career in this industry. Now you're all caught up!
